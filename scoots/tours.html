Tour Cozumel

Want to visit the third largest island in Mexico? Known as the world's second-largest barrier reef, stretching out for nearly 700 miles. With pristine waters and perfect waves that will make any suffer jealous, look no further than Cozumel Island!

Enjoy a peaceful stay in the rich natural beauty of Cozumel. Experience this beauty via boat tours, to hiking through the lush jungle to discover the Mayan ruins or simply immerse yourself into the waters by snorkeling or diving - look out for exotic fish, turtles and dolphins. After a long day of countless activities, sit down to a mouth watering taste of local Mexico.

Our Destinations


Visit the archeological site San Gervasio in Cozumel. Learn about the Mayan heritage and explore the ancient living of this great culture

Stop at a magnificent cave, right by the archeological site San Gervasio. See bats in the cave structures and learn about the geological nature of the Yucatan peninsula and Cozumel.

Go snorkeling amongst Cozumel's beautiful coral reefs just off of the El Cid La Ceiba Beach Hotel.

W02 Assignment: Website Planning Document - The Weather Site

Site Name:WeatherTogether

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the site is to provide a weekly weather forecast for Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven and Storm Center, each day updating the forecast according to a third party forecast provider. The site will be a community where events and activities are recommended based on the weather and users can discuss other events and activities.

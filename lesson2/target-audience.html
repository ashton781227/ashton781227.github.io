Target Audience and Scenarios

Target Audience:

The target audience for this site is individuals between the ages of 18 and 50. The target audience is mostly middle class and upper middle class families, and some lower middle class. The target demographic is adventurous, entertainment seeking, and outdoorsy. Moslty families and younger single people are targeted. Most visitors will be visiting the site on mobile devices, during the day.

Two personas:


Name: Bob Wigglesworth

Age: 28

Occupation: Car mechanic

Demographic: Husband and father of 3. Middle class family.

Goals for using site: Find out the forecast for the weekend and possibly find a fun and affordable outing for the family.

Technology: Apple Iphone, Computer

Scenarios


Scenario 1: A family of four is planning on going to the beach for the day. They want to check the weather to see if the conditions are good, and if there are any beaches that have events on so that they can avoid them as they don't want crowds of people.

Scenario 2: A young single adult is looking for a fun music festival to attend. They want something that fits their age group and wont be too costly. They also want to know how they should dress based on the weather as they want to attend a outdoor function.

Scenario 3: A couple is looking to get fit. They want to start going for runs and want to see what days of the week have good weather, and if there are any runner groups that they can join.

Scenario 4: Two families want to go camping together for the weekend. They want to find a good camp site that friendly for the kids and i a location with good weather.

Scenario 5: A Mother and Father are looking for a sport club for their so to join. They want either soccer or cricket, and would like to find a club for him to join in their area. They are thinking of finding a game to attend first to see if their son enjoys it. They are checking for any games on during the weekend.
